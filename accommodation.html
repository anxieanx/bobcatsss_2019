Accommodation

Please note: Participants are responsible for their own accommodation arrangements.
To save your time, we have picked a lovely and suitable accommodation place for you.

Hotel Osijek ****

At the heart of Osijek's pedestrian zone one can find Hotel Osijek, situated next to a quiet river port and promenade, the inevitable motives of the cities postcards. Its offer will bring a touch of luxury into your every journey, whether you are a meticulous business guest or interested in exploring the regions tourist attractions. In addition to accommodation facilities, hotel offers 5 conference rooms for up to 450 people, and is completely equipped for hosting congresses, business meetings and all kinds of events, with a top quality service.The quality of hotel services, preparation and serving food and beverages, in as well as outside of the hotel, is guaranteed by the implementation of HACCP system and the certificate for control system EN ISO 9001:2000.

The Hotel is certified as Sustainable Hotel by UPUHH (Association of Employers in Croatian Hospitality) and it satisfies high standards regulated by green criteria.

Hotel Osijek has 140 rooms and 7 apartments. According to their quality, all accommodation units are classified as Classic, Superior and Deluxe.
