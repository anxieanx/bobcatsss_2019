Hotel Osijek ****

At the heart of Osijek's pedestrian zone one can find Hotel Osijek, situated next to a quiet river port and promenade, the inevitable motives of the cities postcards. Its offer will bring a touch of luxury into your every journey, whether you are a meticulous business guest or interested in exploring the regions tourist attractions. In addition to accommodation facilities, hotel offers 5 conference rooms for up to 450 people, and is completely equipped for hosting congresses, business meetings and all kinds of events, with a top quality service. Hotel Osijek has 140 rooms and 7 apartments. According to their quality, all accommodation units are classified as Classic, Superior and Deluxe.
