Cancellation Policy and Refunds

Non-author Refund

Refund request should be made via email. Cancellations placed before December 14th, 2018 will be subject to a 20% EUR cancellation fee. NO REFUNDS will be made after December 14th, 2018. Cancellation request must be made in writing by email and sent to bobcatsss2019@gmail.com with subject line: BOBCATSSS 2019 CANCELLATION.

Author Refund

Author registration is non-refundable. Registration fees can be transferred to another co-author under special circumstances. All transfer requests must be made in writing by email and sent to bobcatsss2019@gmail.com with subject line: BOBCATSSS 2019 CANCELLATION.

Author No Shows

BOBCATSSS 2019 enforces a strict “no show” policy. Each accepted submission must be presented by at least one of the authors in-person at the conference site according to the schedule published. Any paper accepted into the program, but not presented on-site will be withdrawn from the official program.

 

IMPORTANT: Bank transfer cost should be paid to the bank separately by the participant.

Please, don’t forget to e-mail the bank transfer receipt to bobcatsss2019@gmail.com with subject line: BOBCATSSSS 2019 REGISTRATION RECEIPT.

The participation fees are owed upon registration and should be paid within 5 days following submission of the registration (but not later than January 8th, 2019).
